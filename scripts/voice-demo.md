## **How Voice Works**

1. **Click "🎤 Speak" button** in the navigation
2. **Speak naturally** - no need for exact phrases
3. **Nexus processes** your command automatically
4. **Click "🔊 Speak"** to hear Nexus respond

Nexus speaks with:
- **Rate**: 0.9 (slightly slower for clarity)
- **Pitch**: 0.8 (deeper, authoritative)
- **Volume**: 0.8 (comfortable listening)

## **Troubleshooting**

### **🎤 Microphone Not Working**
- Check browser permissions
- Ensure microphone is not muted
- Try refreshing the page

### **🔊 Voice Not Speaking**
- Check system volume
- Try clicking "Speak" button again
- Some browsers have autoplay restrictions

### **🧠 Commands Not Recognized**
- Speak clearly and naturally
- Try alternative phrasing
- Check browser console for errors
